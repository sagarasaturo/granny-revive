# Sound & Atmosphere Design (Version 1.0)

Sound is the main horror element in the game.
Inspired by classic Granny gameplay.

## Ambient Sounds

- Wind outside the house
- Light floor creaks
- Distant house noises
- Occasional pipes or wood sounds

Plays at very low volume.

---

## Player Sounds

- Walking: quiet
- Running: loud
- Dropping items: very loud
- Opening doors fast: loud

All sounds can alert Granny.

---

## Granny Sounds

- Footsteps (directional)
- Door opening
- Low breathing or humming
- Alert sound when chasing

Sound volume increases when close.

---

## Puzzle Sounds

- Tool usage makes noise
- Metal sounds are louder
- Puzzle mistakes increase danger

---

## Silence Rules

- Silence increases tension
- No constant music
- Music only during chase

---

## Mobile Audio Rules

- Mono-friendly
- Low file size
- No loud spikes
- Headphone-friendly

---

## Horror Design Notes

- Player hears Granny before seeing her
- Sound creates fear, not jumpscares
- Quiet moments are the scariest
