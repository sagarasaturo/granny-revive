# House Layout (Version 1.0)

The game takes place inside a single large house.
Inspired by the classic Granny layout.

## Main Areas

### Ground Floor
- Entrance Hall
- Living Room
- Kitchen
- Dining Room
- Bathroom

Purpose:
- Early exploration
- Basic items
- Introduction to Granny sounds

---

### Upper Floor
- Bedroom 1
- Bedroom 2
- Storage Room
- Balcony / Hallway

Purpose:
- Keys and notes
- Riskier area
- More Granny patrols

---

### Basement
- Basement Room
- Storage Cage
- Escape-related items

Purpose:
- High risk
- Loud sounds
- End-game puzzles

---

## Room Design Rules

- Each room has at least one hiding spot
- Multiple doors create escape routes
- Narrow hallways increase tension
- Important items never spawn in the same place every game

---

## Navigation Rules

- Doors can be opened slowly or fast
- Some doors are locked
- Shortcuts unlock later
- Player can get lost easily (intended)

---

## Horror Design Notes

- House feels old and abandoned
- Creaky floors in key areas
- Darkness hides Granny movement
- Silence is dangerous
