# Escape Ending (Version 1.0)

The goal of the game is to escape the house.
The ending is tense, slow, and rewarding.

## Escape Method

- Player must unlock the Main Exit Door
- Requires multiple steps:
  1. Find Main Door Key
  2. Disable door lock mechanism
  3. Open exit quietly or risk chase

---

## Final Area

- Exit is located on the Ground Floor
- Area has limited hiding spots
- Granny patrols nearby more often
- High tension zone

---

## Ending Trigger

- When player opens the exit door:
  - Short slow-motion moment
  - Granny sound plays in distance
  - Screen fades out

---

## Victory Screen

- Text: "You Escaped"
- Playtime shown
- Difficulty shown
- Simple dark background
- No loud music

---

## Failure Near Exit

- Loud escape actions alert Granny
- If caught near exit:
  - Player restarts from checkpoint
  - No instant win allowed

---

## Design Notes

- Escape should feel earned
- No easy shortcuts
- Fear until the last second
